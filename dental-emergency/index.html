Dental Emergency

Dental emergencies are situations where you encounter severe tooth pain, severe infection, uncontrolled bleeding, or traumatic dental injuries that need immediate treatment.

Asia Pacific Dental Centre is committed to serve our patients and provide urgent care when needed. Please call us at +65 8646 9872 and our team members will advise you.

Get In Touch
How does emergency dental work?
  • Call our Dental Emergency Hotline +65 8646 9872 for emergency services.
  • Our Operator will get in touch with you and explain the activation charges.
  • Once payment has been made, our designated emergency team will be activated.
